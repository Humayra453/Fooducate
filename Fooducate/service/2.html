School Speakers and Workshops

HEALTHIER AND HAPPIER CHILDREN & TEENAGERS

Our handpicked expert team of Nutritionists are passionate about educating young people on the importance of self-care through healthy lifestyle habits and improved food choices. We have been hosting the Food for Life talks and workshops for over 12 years and talk to over 20,000 students & parents each year, visiting schools and working with many of the top 200 schools. We support all the schools we work with via regular blogs on hot topics of interest throughout the year which are geared for teenagers and their parents.

To complement our talks, we also offer the Fooducate Teenage Wellness Handbook, as well as books for parents and The University Wellness Handbook. Do get in touch if you'd like us to visit your school. We can create bespoke talks and workshops for your requirements.
